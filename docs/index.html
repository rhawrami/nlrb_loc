NLRB Case Locations

This map displays currently open National Labor Relations Board cases across the country (with recent activity in at least 2024 or 2025). Using initial case data from the NLRB Research database, I then merge with the official case pages to display various case data, from the inital filing date to the allegations. For more extensive and proper querying, please visit the NLRB Research database.

To start, click on any marker to see the associated details of the case, with hyperlinks to documents when available. From there, you can query cases by their name and/or location, like "Starbucks" or "Sacramento," and/or click on the filter icon to filter out cases based on document type, like looking at only ALJ decisions. You can also zoom in and out of the frame, as well as make the map full-screen.

This map will update once a week. If you'd like to give any feedback, contact me below. Thanks!
